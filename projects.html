About AI Classroom Assistants

We are partnering with the Department for Education to pilot and evaluate edtech tools in schools, to assess their impact on pupil outcomes, and collect large-scale data.

Schools and colleges will trial and evaluate digital and AI tools to understand what works best for teaching and learning. This will provide real pupil interaction data which is critical for the creation of the sovereign benchmarks.
